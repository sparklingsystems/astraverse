Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to some of your common questions.

What is Astraverse?

Astraverse is a social media app for astrophotography enthusiasts to share their astro images, connect with fellow enthusiasts and stay updated on latest astronomy events.

Is Astraverse available on other platforms?

Currently, Astraverse is available only as an Android app. We are working on expanding our app to other platforms in the future. Stay tuned for updates!

How can I search for specific posts or users?

Currently, the search function for posts is limited to exact tags, and for user names, it requires the starting string of the username. We know this is very limiting, but we are working on improving the search functionality to make it more flexible and comprehensive.

How is the Feed on Home Tab displayed?

The feed displays latest or trending posts from the Astraverse community. You can select from Trending/Latest from the button on top right.

What is the "Community" tab for?

The "Community" tab is where you can connect with other enthusiasts, ask questions, and participate in discussions.

What news can I find in the "News" section?

The "News" section is currently under development. We will update it with relevant astronomy news and articles soon.

Who owns the copyright to the images I upload?

You retain full ownership and copyright of all images you upload to Astraverse. By posting, you grant Astraverse a non-exclusive license to display and share your images within the app.

When I try to upload an image, the app says that this image can't be uploaded for now.

Currently we support astronomy based images only, any irrevelant image is not allowed to prevent spam.

Why should I add tags?

Tags will help other users to find your post by searching for relevant keywords, because search is currently supported only by tags.

Why should I add my equipments?

Adding equipments will help you quickly select the rig you used to capture a particular image (if you fill the advanced details section while uploading an image).

How can I report a bug or provide feedback?

Go to settings and select "Report Bug/Submit Feedback" to provide feedback. Your feedback is always welcome.
