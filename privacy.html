Privacy Policy

Welcome to Astraverse! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Astraverse collects some personal information from you when you register on the app. This includes

  • name
  • username
  • email address
  • profile photo (if you upload one)

Astraverse also uses Firebase crashlytics to analyze app crash reports for improving stability and performance, and Google Analytics to understand user behavior and improve the app. Such data collected may include

  • Session duration
  • Operating systems
  • Device models
  • Approximate Location
  • First launches
  • App opens

In addition, we also collect the images and related content data uploaded by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

Name, Username, Email, User Bio

  • Your name, email, username, and profile photo (if provided) are used for account creation and management purposes,
  • Your name, username, bio, profile photo (if provided) is displayed publicly on your profile.

User-Uploaded Astro Images

  • For displaying and sharing astro images within the community
  • Your images are visible to other users to facilitate discussions, feedback, and appreciation within the community.
  • You control what astro-images you upload, and uploading any astro image is completely optional.

As stated, we utilize Firebase Crashlytics and Google Analytics to enhance your experience with Astraverse by analyzing app usage and performance. Here’s how the information collected may be used

Firebase Crashlytics

  • App Stability: We analyze crash reports to identify and fix bugs, ensuring a stable app experience.
  • Performance Improvement: Insights from Crashlytics help us optimize performance on different devices and operating systems.
  • You control what astro-images you upload, and uploading any astro image is completely optional.

Google Analytics

  • User Behavior: We gather data on session duration, operating systems, device models, approximate location, first launches, and app opens.
  • App Enhancement: This data helps us understand how users interact with Astraverse, allowing us to make informed decisions to improve features and usability.

3. Third-Party Services

Astraverse utilizes services by Google like Firebase, and Analytics. These services collect and process users' information like name, username, email, and other user-generated data, and other analytics and crash report data as governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these services to understand how their data is handled.
Third party services used are

  • Firebase for authentication, database, and storage,
  • Google Analytics for app analytics information,
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting.

4. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We store all data, including personal information and uploaded content, in Firebase servers, a secure database provided by Google. Data is encrypted both in transit using HTTPS and at rest using encryption mechanisms provided by Firebase. For more detailed information on Firebase security practices, please refer to Firebase's Security and Privacy documentation here.
